## About

I'm a **Data Scientist and AI/ML Engineer** who builds intelligent systems at the intersection of language models, analytics, and decision-making. My focus is on practical, deployable AI — especially for **low-resource environments** where compute is constrained but impact needs to be high.

My work spans LLM fine-tuning and RAG architectures, predictive analytics, monitoring & evaluation systems, and full-stack AI-powered applications. I care deeply about turning complex research into tools that actually work in the field.
